  WEBSITE PURPOSE & BUSINESS OBJECTIVE
  ──────────────────────────────────────
  AdhritVerse is a tech consultancy positioned to serve both early-stage
  startups and established enterprises. The website is architected with a
  clear goal: to attract qualified leads, demonstrate technical expertise
  through a curated portfolio, and convert first-time visitors into clients
  through strategically placed conversion touchpoints across all five pages.

  The combination of cybersecurity expertise and modern web/app development
  services positions AdhritVerse as a comprehensive digital partner rather
  than a single-service vendor.
